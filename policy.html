Quality Policy

EF Architects and Engineers Consulting Plc is a company that provides high standard, cost efficient, quality and creative nature Architectural and engineering services to the construction industry by realistic cost, on timely bases and to high satisfaction of customers.
This will be achieved by:
  • Implementing ISO 9001:2008 QMS
  • Communicating the spirit to employee
  • Improving competence of employee through training and Effective Communication
  • Collecting feedback from customer
  • Continual improvement of Quality Management System.
  • Abiding applicable regulatory requirements and ethical norms
  • Making comfortable working environment with high aesthetic standards
  • Motivating employee by apprising
